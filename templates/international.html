International Awareness Project

The spring of 2016, I worked with two other students to address a gap between international and domestic students at the University of Virginia. As the world becomes increasingly globalized, we recognize the importance in understanding the similarities and differences between cultures. Through our project, we hope participants can learn a new persepective and make a new friend.
